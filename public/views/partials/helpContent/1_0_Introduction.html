1  Introduction

ONEmSimulator is a web-based simulation tool that allows the creation and simulation "semi-static" ONEm services. The simulator is in no way connected to the ONEm trial or live platforms, it is indended to help the service designer to visualize what the end-service might look like when finally implemented on the ONEm platform "proper".

The simulator can be used by internal ONEm staff, by mobile operators or by third parties wishing to understand the "look and feel" and "user experience" aspects of ONEm services.
